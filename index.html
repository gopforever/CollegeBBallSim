Welcome! This MVP simulates a full D-I style season using real team names from data/teams.csv (you can replace that file with a complete list). It creates conference schedules, non-conf games, conference tournaments, and a national bracket sized to your universe (8/16/32/64).

Everything auto-saves to your browser. Use Export Save before clearing your cache or moving devices.

Note: Real team names are trademarks of their respective schools/conferences. This tool is for educational/fan use and is not affiliated with the NCAA or any institution.
